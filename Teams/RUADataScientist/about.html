What is it all about?

Data science is not a new term in the world of technology today. It has taken over the corporate world in the last few years. The demand for skilled data science professionals has seen an upsurge, as organizations are on the constant lookout for data science professionals to resolve business complexities with efficient data analysis. This website introduces absolute data science beginners to the profession. In our topic area, you find articles to all the buzzwords spread by media, as well as deep dives and links for further research. Feel free to test yourself with our Personality-Test.

Our Data-Science Test

The entire project is non-profit, making the test 100% free for you. The Test consists out of a diverse set of questions related to multiple topics within and around data Science. You will not only test your knowledge, but also get to answer questions that specificaly aim to find out if Data Science is the right thing for you. All of this might be confusing, but don't be scared. We always keep in mind how confusing it was for us in the beginning.
