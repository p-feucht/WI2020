Fundamentals

As a Data Scientist you need some basics. For your career it is necessary to know the basics in programming, math and in economics.

Programming

As a Data Scientist you must know the basic of programming. For that you should have some knowledge in a basic language like Java or C. Also some skills in Html, CSS, Java Script and PHP are nice to have to develop am own website if needed. But the most important programming language to know as a Data Scientist is Python or R . Both languages are used to develop machine learning algorithms, analyse data or visualize data. How you can see in the picture, SQL is the most used language as a Data Scientist. SQL is necessary to sort, collect and connect Data. SQL is a MUST-HAVE in the Data Science world like Python or R.

Mathematics and Statistics

As a Data Scientist you should have a basic understanding of logic, algebra, analysis and statistics. They are necessary when you have to work with data Warehouse software or most important when you analyse Data and try to visualize your findings. When you want to interpret a graph or other visualizations of Data, you will be grateful for your statistic knowledge.

Economics

For your Job you have to know the basics of economics. This means a basic understanding about important numbers for the business, how a business runs, how the market is developing and how the market is influenced by different parties. All this is necessary to understand why your job is important and to understand the questions you have to solve as a data scientist.
